Australia Labour Force Visualization

This interactive web page presents various visualizations reflecting trends and patterns in the Australian labour force. Below you will find detailed maps and charts that illustrate the employment landscape across different states and territories.

Employment Trends by State

The map above displays the number of employed people in each Australian state as of January 2024. This choropleth map helps to visually compare employment levels across regions, highlighting areas with higher or lower employment rates. Navigate over each state to see specific employment data.

Monthly Unemployment Trends

This line chart shows the trends in unemployment rates from 1966 to the latest available data in 2024. It tracks changes in unemployment rates across Australia, providing insights into economic cycles and job market dynamics.
